    More about online MBA

    What are the benefits of pursuing an online MBA in India?

    Flexibility: Online MBA courses provide utmost flexibility, allowing learners to access course materials and lectures at their convenience. This allows them to balance their studies with work or other commitments.

    Affordability: Generally, online MBA courses in India are more cost-effective when compared to traditional MBA degrees.

    Choose from top universities: Learners can study from top universities worldwide without having to travel or relocate.

    Professional networking: Online MBA programs often facilitate networking through virtual forums, discussion boards, and collaborative projects. This allows students to connect with peers, professors, and professionals from diverse backgrounds, creating valuable networking opportunities.

    Why choose Online Manipal for your online MBA?

    Fast-tracked career progression: The online MBA courses offered by Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ), Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), and Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) allow you to explore opportunities to secure lucrative positions in various domains and sectors. Enhance your organizational impact by assuming high paying roles in your chosen domain. 58% of graduates at MUJ remarked that their online PG degree boosted their chances of getting hired.

    Career-oriented business skills: You will master skills like decision making, problem solving, and networking to manage & solve organizational challenges. 73%of learners felt they were able to gain in-depth knowledge in their domain or field

    Wide range of electives/specializations: You can choose to pursue your online MBA in many specialized domains like finance, HR, data science, business analytics, project management, healthcare management, and more.

    Global networking & access to alumni: You can network with a diverse student community and industry leaders to learn about market challenges and trends from around the world. After the completion of the program, you will also get the prestigious Manipal alumni status.

    Advanced digital platform: From live classes, recorded sessions, e-learning material, e-books and other course material, students can access everything at one place- the advanced learning management system (LMS). The LMS is also available as a mobile app, which can be accessed from anywhere and works even on low bandwidth.

    Online proctored exams: Students can write online proctored exams from the comfort of their homes by booking convenient slots.

    What are the eligibility criteria for online MBA?

    Our online MBA programs are designed for freshers looking to pursue a masters degree and working professionals with various commitments looking to upskill and transition into managerial positions. To learn more about the eligibility criteria, you can check out MUJs online MBA , MAHEs online MBA for working professionals , and SMU’s online MBA with dual specialization.

    Do I need to get a high CAT score to get admission into an online MBA program?

    No, it is not compulsory to get a high CAT score for getting admission into any online MBA program offered through Online Manipal. For admission into Manipal University Jaipur’s (MUJ) online MBA, candidates should either have a valid CAT score (40 percentile) or should clear the University’s aptitude test. A CAT score is not mandatory even for MAHE’s and SMU’s online MBA programs.

     

    Here are the details of Manipal online MBA programs

    What are the various MBA electives/specializations offered?

    Through our online MBA programs at Online Manipal, you can explore in-demand electives/specializations like Finance, Marketing, HR, Analytics & Data Science, IT & FinTech, Operations Management, International Business, Information System Management, Project Management, Supply Chain Management, Data Science, Business Analytics, Healthcare Management, and Pharmaceutical Management.

    What are the various scholarships offered?

    Scholarship opportunities are offered to learners in various categories- Merit, defense personnel, differently abled persons, government employees, sportspersons, and more. For more details on scholarship opportunities, you can check out MUJs online MBA ,MAHEs online MBA for working professionals , and online MBA with dual specialization from SMU

    What are the job roles you can explore after online MBA in India?

    The job roles may vary depending on the university, elective/specialization, and the individual’s potential. However, here are some of the top jobs that can be explore post an online MBA- Product Manager,Lead Data Scientist, Marketing Manager, Operations Lead, HR Director, Digital Marketer, Business Analyst, Supply Chain Manager, Asset Manager, Quality Controller, Entrepreneurship, and more. For learners choosing to pursue MBA in Banking & Financial Services, some of the top jobs to explore are Investment Banker, Credit Risk Analyst, Wealth Manager, Asset Manager, Financial Risk Manager, Financial Analyst, and more.

    Does the online MBA program come with placement assistance?

    Yes. Learners will receive various placement assistance in the form of skill assessments & enhancement, industry-readiness sessions, resume building, mock interviews, and virtual placement drives.

    Are Manipal universities a good option to pursue a business degree?

    Yes, Manipal universities in India are among the top-ranked institutions in the NIRF’s management category. TAPMI, the business school under MAHE, ranks 42nd in the management category and is considered one of the best B-schools in India. Manipal University Jaipur holds the 73rd position among management institutions in India.

    Beyond rankings and accreditations, Manipal B-schools boast a distinguished alumni network excelling in various fields. One-fifth of TAPMI alumni hold CXO positions in top companies like JP Morgan Chase & Co, American Express, PwC, and BMW.

    At Manipal University Jaipur, 75% of the Faculty of Management & Commerce members hold PhDs. Alumni from MUJ’s business schools work at leading global companies, including Amazon, Microsoft, Deutsche Bank, JSW Steel, and UltraTech Cement.

    These rankings and the extensive alumni base underscore the quality of Manipal universities, establishing them as some of the top online MBA schools in India.
