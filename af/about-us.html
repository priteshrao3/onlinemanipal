Our heritage

Padma Shri Dr. T. M. A. Pai
(1898- 1979)

"Give an individual a professional education and you have given him a lifestyle."

The roots of Manipal Group date back to 1953, when our founder Dr. T.M.A. Pai set up Kasturba Medical College on a barren hill on the outskirts of the town of Udupi in the State of Karnataka. In the subsequent years, many other academic institutions followed suit and this vibrant campus became the town of Manipal.


The MEMG group has been synonymous with excellence in higher education with various world-class universities like Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) & Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) and top-ranked institutes like Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), Kasturba Medical College (KMC) & T A Pai Management Institute (TAPMI). These educational institutions have created and nurtured bright minds who are now creating sustainable real-world impact across the world.

About Online Manipal

Online Manipal is the digital home for all Manipal universities that are NAAC accredited and offer UGC-entitled online degrees that are considered at par with on-campus degrees. Since its inception in 2021, Online Manipal has empowered over 50,000 learners to realize their potential by providing them with accessible, affordable & industry-relevant degrees and new-age certifications in a 100% online mode.
