Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)

Download Brochure

Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) is a UGC-approved university that has redefined excellence in higher education over the years. A NAAC A+ accredited university, Manipal University Jaipur has stood as a pioneer, paving the way for industry-focused education. A multi-disciplinary university, Manipal University Jaipur inspires learners across all disciplines to study and innovate through strong theoretical knowledge & practical, hands-on experience.

 

In 2021, Manipal University Jaipur started a new chapter when the University Grants Commission (UGC) permitted the university to offer online degree programs, with the first cohort starting in April 2021. Online degrees from Manipal University Jaipur are taught by world-class faculty and have gained popularity among both freshers and working professionals from different regions and countries.
