Sikkim Manipal University (SMU)

Download Brochure

Sikkim Manipal University (SMU) was established in 1995 with an aim of reinforcing the strong connection between education & skill development, empowering the people of Sikkim, and contributing to the human development in the Northeast and other Indian states.

 

Sikkim Manipal University is a NAAC A+ accredited university and its degrees are UGC-entitled. SMU’s distance education arm – founded in 2001, has educated over 500,000 students, becoming a top provider of professional programs in India. Recognized for its excellence, including the ‘Most Preferred Distance Education University Among Students’ award, SMU now offers affordable online degree programs, further expanding its commitment to accessible education.

    Learning methodology
    Lumen: An AI-powered mobile-first learning platform

    Our AI-driven platform, Lumen, offers interactive, personalized, and gamified learning experiences. Being user-friendly and adaptive, it empowers learners across the world.

    Lumen gives access to live classroom sessions, audio-video content, discussion forums, notes, assignments, practice quizzes, and real time support from subject matter experts.

    LumenAI breaks down concepts in an understandable manner; it also quizzes the learners on the topic to reinforce learning.

    AI-powered nudge engine and mobile widgets send notifications through calls, SMS & WhatsApp to ensure online learning is on track for their live classes, assignments, quizzes, discussion forums, and overall content progress.
