Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)

Download Brochure

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is synonymous with excellence in higher education. Over 33,000 students from 67 different nations live, learn and play in the sprawling university town, nestled on a plateau in Karnataka’s Udupi district. MAHE has nearly 2,700 faculty, who cater to the various professional institutions in health sciences, engineering, management, communication, and humanities.

 

Manipal Academy of Higher Education is one of India’s leading academic and research institutions with a NAAC A++ rating and prestigious Institution of Eminence (IoE) recognition. The institution has been offering world-class education across diverse streams since 1953. It is ranked #4 by NIRF and has a track of notable alumni like Satya Nadella, Vikas Khanna, and Dr Devi Prasad Shetty.

 

MAHE has also begun offering various in-demand master’s programs and professional certifications in the online mode to professionals across the world.
